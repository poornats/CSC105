Compliance

Team 2, Week 1

Compliance percentages with the Garmin wearable of participants in Team 2;
Each day is split into 48 slots of 30 min each. A score of 1 or 0 is assigned for each participant for each time window on each day.
If a wearable data entry exists for a participant in a time window, a score of 1 is assigned. Compliance percentage for a
day is calculated as (sum of scores for the day)/48*100
